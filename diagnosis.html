The Diagnosis

A free church health check

The Diagnosis is a free, five-minute assessment that shows an Australian church where it is stretched — across communications, admin and scheduling, technology, compliance, and pastoral care. You answer a short set of questions, get an honest read on screen straight away, and one practical thing to try this week. No cost, no obligation.

What happens after

From a five-minute check to a church that runs itself

  1. You take the Diagnosis

    Five minutes, on any device. You get your results immediately, with one thing to try this week — whether or not we ever speak.

  2. We talk, if it's useful

    If the results point to something worth doing together, we have a no-pressure conversation. If they don't, that's a good outcome too.

  3. We co-write an Engagement Brief

    A one-page working strategy, written with your whole team — naming the first win, who owns what, and the rhythm going forward.

  4. You get a Process Guide and run it yourself

    A plain-language how-to in your church's own style, so your team keeps the new system running long after EZER steps back.

Common questions

Before you start

How long does it take?

About five minutes. It works well on a phone between other things.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. It's free, with no obligation to work with EZER afterwards.

What happens to my answers?

They generate your own results on screen. If you choose to share an email, we send a copy plus a free AI-for-churches guide. Answers may be aggregated anonymously to build Australian church benchmarks — never sold or shared individually.

Do I have to commit to anything?

No. The Diagnosis is a generous front door. Many churches take it, act on the free suggestions, and never need anything further.
